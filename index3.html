Depression Disorder Visualizations

Number of people with depression in the World

This map shows the number of people suffering from depression in each country in the world in 2017, and the comparison of colors shows that China and India are the countries with more people suffering from depression than other countries.


Depression Disorder by Age in Australia

This chart emphasis on the age distribution of Australian patients in 2017. Different colors are used to show the stratified number rate at different ages. Specific figures are also marked behind each bar for the audience to view and analyze.


Depression Disorder by Gender in Australia

Different from the previous charts, this chart shows in detail the number of male and female affected in Australia from 1990 to 2017, and also there is an upward trend in the number of cases these years.


Suicide Rate vs. Prevalence Rate

This chart shows the prevalence of depression and suicide rates in Australia over 27 years. Viewers can also use a slider to select the year they want to look at for analysis, different colored circles represent different years.
